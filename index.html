01 / OVERVIEW

Scientific Narrative & Research Mission

As a postgraduate researcher in Applied Mathematics and Optimization, I develop mathematically rigorous optimization methods for machine learning. My research focuses on Gap-Safe screening rules for high-dimensional optimization, enabling faster and more scalable algorithms for sparse regression, signal recovery, and related optimization problems.

Optimization Theory

Formulating fast dual screening tests, dual gap analyses, and primal-dual algorithms to accelerate sparse regressions (LASSO, Group LASSO).

Machine Learning Foundations

Strengthening support vector machines, neural network convergence proofs, and generalization bounds using analytical metrics.

Data Science

Applying statistical modeling, machine learning algorithms, and high-dimensional data analysis to extract actionable insights and optimize predictive performance.
