01 / OVERVIEW

Scientific Narrative & Research Mission

As a dedicated postgraduate researcher in Applied Mathematics and Optimization, my scientific vision lies at the intersection of mathematical theory and computer science. My research explores how classical principles of mathematical analysis and multi-valued mapping theory can be synthesized to formulate reliable, mathematically rigorous algorithms for complex machine learning tasks.

I focus on developing Gap-Safe screening rules for high-dimensional dual optimization models. These frameworks accelerate computation times significantly by safely discarding irrelevant coordinates, rendering optimization models highly scalable for sparse regression, signal recovery, and variational problems.

Optimization Theory

Formulating fast dual screening tests, dual gap analyses, and primal-dual algorithms to accelerate sparse regressions (LASSO, Group LASSO).

Machine Learning Foundations

Strengthening support vector machines, neural network convergence proofs, and generalization bounds using analytical metrics.

Variational Analysis

Investigating structural regularities of collections of set-valued mappings and metric subregularities in non-convex optimization.
